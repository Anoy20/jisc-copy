Bangladesh is a diverse tourist destination, offering attractions like the Sundarbans mangrove forest, the world's longest natural beach at Cox's Bazar, the tea gardens of Sreemangal and Sylhet, and historical sites in Dhaka like Lalbagh Fort and the Ahsan Manzil Museum. Other popular spots include St. Martin's Island, the hills of Bandarban and Rangamati, and the archaeological site of Paharpur.
